About Us

The company is private limited company that was registered on the 28th day of July, 1992 under the COMPANIES AND ALLIED MATTERS DECREE of 1990 with the CORPORATE AFFAIRS COMMISSION of Nigeria. It is headed by a Board of Directors with only the Ogunpa office at its inception. It started operations with some members of staff. Presently we are proud to boast of four different branches with daily business activities in all the branches. Our services ranges from General Merchandising, Machines & Machinery, Tools, Manufacturer's representative and so on.
